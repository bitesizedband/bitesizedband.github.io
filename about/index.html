about

we’re an indie duo based in switzerland.

we started making music in 2021, after tobias made a post on a local music website looking to start a band, and ana replied.

we produce our music ourselves, with tobias taking the guitar and backing vocal duties and ana doing the singing, playing the keys and the electric cello.

if you want to say hi, we would love to hear from you. you can contact us on social media or via email: hi@bitesizedband.com
